Design principles

  • Map tightly to ACM CS1 outcomes: fundamentals, reading Python, strings/files, graphics, classes.
  • Intentional ambiguity: underspecify formats/representations to provoke design decisions.
  • Visual specification: use images/pixels (harder for LLMs trained on text).
  • Interdependence: later milestones depend on earlier representations/logic.
  • Cultural/ethical context: computing connected to lived realities (Andes, data centers, extraction).
Ambiguity is pivotal: AI solves well-specified, templated tasks; ambiguity foregrounds modeling, interpretation, and judgment.
